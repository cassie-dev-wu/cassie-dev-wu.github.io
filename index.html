ABOUT
ME

      
    
        
  • 
          
    What's your story?
    
          
    I build AI tools for people who aren't developers. Most AI products today are too complex, too generic, or built by engineers who've never felt the problem themselves. I build from the other side — starting with the friction, then engineering the solution.
    
        
    • 
        
  • 
          
    What are you building right now?
    
          
    Right now: WorkPilot — an AI assistant for young professionals navigating workplace communication. The gap between what people can do and how they present themselves professionally is massive. WorkPilot closes that gap.
    
        
    • 
        
  • 
          
    Why this direction?
    
          
    The best products are built by people who feel the problem before they understand the solution. I don't research user pain points — I've lived them. That's a different kind of conviction, and it shows in the product.
    
        
    • 
        
  • 
          
    What's your technical background?
    
          
    Full-stack across TypeScript, React, Next.js, Node.js, Python and AI/ML tooling. I care equally about engineering and design — I believe the best products are indistinguishable from great craft.
    
        
    • 
        
  • 
          
    How do you think about AI?
    
          
    As infrastructure, not magic. The real opportunity isn't building another chatbot — it's wrapping AI's raw power in an interface so simple that anyone can get results without understanding what's underneath.
